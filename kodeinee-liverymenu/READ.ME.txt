# kodeinee-liverymenu

A simple QBCore resource that adds a `liverymenu` command to open a menu for selecting and applying vehicle liveries.

---

## Features

- Opens a menu to select from available liveries on the vehicle you're currently inside.
- Applies the selected livery instantly.
- Simple and lightweight, depends on QBCore and qb-menu.

---

## Installation

1. Place the `kodeinee-liverymenu` folder into your server's `resources` directory.

2. Add the following line to your `server.cfg` (make sure it loads after `qb-core` and `qb-menu`)

```plaintext
ensure kodeinee-liverymenu
